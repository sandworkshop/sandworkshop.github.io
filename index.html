Call for Abstracts

The goal of SAND is to discuss statistical challenges that arise with state-of-the-art neuronal recording technologies. We seek to foster discussion across computational and experimental neuroscience, in particular about experiments and data which are pushing the boundaries of what is currently possible (e.g., in terms of the number of neurons recorded, spatial & temporal resolution of the recordings, stimulation paradigms, behavioral paradigms, etc).

We invite the community to submit 1 page abstracts on recent projects that are relevant to these goals. Please submit your abstracts through this portal

Submission Deadline: March 21 2025 (anywhere on earth)

Author Notification: April 4 2025
